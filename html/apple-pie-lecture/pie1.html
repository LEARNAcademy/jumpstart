Ingredients

  • Large Granny Smith apples (1/2 inch slices)
  • Large Honeycrisp apples (1/2 inch slices)
  • Large Golden Delicious apples (1/2 inch slices)
  • Pie crusts
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Tapioca
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Lemon (zested and juiced)
  • Large Egg (lightly beaten for egg wash)

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Prebake pie crust inside a deep dish pie pan
  3. Mix all the ingredients except egg wash in a mixing bowl
  4. Spoon the apple filling over the bottom crust and discard juices
  5. Lay a pie crust over the apple filling
  6. Trim the dough along the outside edge of the pie pan
  7. Cut 4 slits in the top of the dough to allow steam to vent
  8. Cover the edges with a strip of foil to keep from over browning
  9. Bake them!
    • 400 degrees for 25 minutes
    • Remove aluminum foil
    • 375 degrees for an additional 30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the juices are bubbly
  10. Let them sit for a bit
